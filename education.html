Education
Here are some useful tools students can use for help with homework, SAT/ACT practice, and learning outside of the school environment.
Khan Academy
-Access to practice tests and practice problems
-Personalized learning in which you can work and practice at your own pace.
- Free online courses and lessons
- Practice tests for the SAT
Quizlet
- Use flashcards, games, and other learning tools to help you study.
- Able to create your own study tools
- 90% of Quizlet users have reported that they’ve received higher grades using this as a study tool.
- Find other people’s flashcards and use them as study guides.
MyStudyLife
- 16 to 24 years old
- 3 month commitment
- 12 to 15 hours/week
- SF minimum wage
- Recieve individual support, skill training, and education support
- Tutoring and snacks
Duolingo
-Learn a new language
-Up to 30+ languages available
-Have time to work a minimum of 6 hours per week
CollegeBoard
-Register for AP, Subject, and SAT tests here
-Research colleges and find ones that best fit you
-Check test scores for the ones you’ve taken
-A website that promotes college readiness
Easy Bib
-Create accurate citations for the bibliographies of research papers
- Cite anything from journals, books, to websites
